
# Momentum User Guide

Welcome to **Momentum**! This guide will help you get the most out of your new life management platform.

## 🚀 Getting Started

### Dashboard Overview
The dashboard is your command center. It gives you a quick snapshot of:
- **Active Habits**: How many habits you are tracking.
- **Completion Rate**: Your consistency percentage.
- **Task completion**: Daily productivity metrics.
- **AI Insights**: Personalized tips based on your activity.

---

## ✅ Habits

### Creating a Habit
1. Navigate to the **Habits** tab.
2. Click **"+ New Habit"**.
3. Enter a name (e.g., "Read 30 mins").
4. Choose a frequency (Daily, Weekly, or specific days).
5. Select a category (Health, Work, Learning, etc.).

### Tracking Progress
- Click the checkbox on the habit card to mark it as **Done** for today.
- View your **Heatmap** in the Analytics tab to see your consistency over time.
- Keep your **Streak** alive! Fire icons show your current run.

---

## 📔 Journal & AI Analysis

### Writing an Entry
1. Go to **Journal**.
2. Click **"+ New Entry"** or select today's date.
3. Start writing freely, or use the templates:
   - **Mistakes Done**: What did you learn?
   - **Good Things**: Gratitude practice.
   - **Tomorrow's Planner**: Plan your next day.

### Voice Journaling
- Click the **Microphone** icon to use voice-to-text.
- Speak naturally; the AI will transcribe your thoughts instantly.

### Mood Tracking
- Select your mood (Great, Good, Okay, Bad, Terrible) for each entry.
- Check the **Mood Chart** in Analytics to see emotional trends.

---

## 📋 Tasks & Planning

### Managing Tasks
- **Add Task**: Quick add from the dashboard or Tasks page.
- **Prioritize**: Set High, Medium, or Low priority.
- **Smart Integration**: Items added to "Tomorrow's Planner" in your Journal automatically become tasks for the next day!

---

## 🤖 AI Personal Assistant

Momentum's AI is here to help you stay organized.

### What can it do?
- **"Create a habit to drink water daily"** -> Instantly creates the habit.
- **"Remind me to call Mom tomorrow"** -> Adds a task with a due date.
- **"How is my productivity looking?"** -> Analyzes your stats and gives a summary.
- **"Analyze my journal for patterns"** -> Reads your recent entries to find insights.

### Privacy
- You explicitly consent to AI data access.
- You can revoke access at any time in **Settings**.

---

## 💡 Pro Tips

- **Crazy Mode**: Toggle the theme switch to "Crazy" for a fun, animated interface.
- **Heatmap**: The more you do (habits + tasks + journals), the greener your heatmap gets!
- **Exports**: You can export all your data to CSV/JSON from the Settings page.

Enjoy building Momentum! 🚀
